Our mission is simple: set a standard worthy of the Spartan name — on the pitch and off it.

Player-first development. Structured coaching, role clarity, and honest feedback to compound skill over ego.

Data with feel. We use video, scoring, and basic analytics to inform decisions — without losing instinct.

Culture that endures. We celebrate effort, protect each other, and keep the room humble, hungry, and together.

How we got here

2021

Foundation

Started as a small pickup group with borrowed gear and a big standard: do everything like it matters.

2023

Program formalized

Three-day training cadence, role-based selection, and a shared playbook for how Spartans compete.

2024

Comp squads & fixtures

Multiple squads, regular competition, and partnerships that helped us scale without losing culture.

2025

Performance flywheel

Video + score tracking to review powerplays, death overs, and strike rotation — learning loops went pro.
