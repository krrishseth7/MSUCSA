MSU Cricket Student Association - Team

Saad Mahmood

Player 1

Country: Pakistan

Position: Batting all-rounder

Saad, originally from Lahore, Pakistan is the new captain of the MSU cricket team for the 2025-26 season. He joined the team in his freshman year, 2022-23 season and slowly solidifed his place in the squad by showing true grit and passion for cricket. His tactial thinking and strong leadership have taken the team to new heights by setting records which seemed impossible, such as setting the target of 270. Now marking the start of his senior year and captaincy, he leads his 15-man squad from the forefront to the many championships that lie ahead in this season.

Arhaan Parikh

Player 2

Country: India and UAE

Position: All-rounder

History: Arhaan, from Dubai, United Arab Emirates is the new vice captain of the MSU cricket team for the 2025-26 season. He marked his strong start in the team with his highlight performances at the National College Cricket Association 2024-25 regional tournament, in Ohio, with one of the highest player points in the tournament while being the youngest. His remarkable half century against Troy Tigers and match winning 45 against Greater Detroit Cricket Club in the Friendship Cup 2025 truly highlight the kind of talent he is. His ability to bring the whole team together and undeniable skill as a pure all-rounder led him to his position as Vice Captain.

Adarsh Vatts

Player 3

Country: India

Position: Batsman

History: Adarsh, originally from Uttar Pradesh, India is the highest run scorer of MSU Cricket. His highlight captaincy season 2024-25 marked the beginning of a new era for the MSU Club Cricket Team after 4 years of not winning any games. Adarsh with his domestic cricket experience from India brought structure into a team that was once all over the place. His achievements as captain also include being runners-up in the Michigan College Cricket Tournament and NCCA regional tournament, bringing home our first trophy in 7 long years. Adarsh's ability to take charge and deal with pressure such as in the NCCA against University Of Michigan while chasing with less wickets in hand all while battling sickness truly showed the team what pure grit and leadership can do.

Manish Kumar

Player 5

Country: India

Position: Opening batsman

History: Manish, originally from Haryana, India is starting his second season with the MSU Cricket Club in 2025-26. Manish is one of our most experienced right handed batsman who has changed the face of opening batting in our team. Dev Jariwala

Player 6

Country: USA

Position: Opening Batsman

History: Dev, originally from Michigan, USA is starting his second season with the MSU Cricket Club in 2025-26. Dev is the backbone of the Spartans Cricket Team and one of the most talented young crickters to come from the state of Michigan. His batting style is a mix of pure technique ,impeccable timing, and the ability to punish the best bowlers. Dev's adaptive batting style has proven to be his most helpful skill for the team helping us in crunch situations while putting up a stabilising knock in the NCCA regionals in 2024-25 against University of Michigan in a nail biting run chase.
